# Manual Downloads

These assets were verified on official Quaternius source pages as CC0/free, but the pages did not expose a direct shell-safe archive URL. They were skipped per the asset-ingestion rule: if a page does not expose a direct safe download link, add it here for manual download.

Do not use mirrors, reposts, paid/source tiers, or scraped files. For each manual download, open the official source page, choose the free/base download path offered by Quaternius, save the untouched archive under the raw target, keep any licence/readme files beside it, then update `asset_manifest.csv` with the exact filename, size, checksum, and final status.
